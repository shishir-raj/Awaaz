Welcome to the Project Awaaz community! Project Awaaz is a completely non-profit attempt to crowd-source audio content for the 15 million visually impaired people in India. And thanks to people like you, we're inching closer to our goal every day! 

We right now have poems in 5 languages [English, Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati & Tamil] and plan to add more literature every day. After careful moderation, the audio-content that you record will be distributed to blind schools, NGOs and other partners working towards the common goal of - Access to content for all. 

Log on to www.projectawaaz.org and be a part of the change!
