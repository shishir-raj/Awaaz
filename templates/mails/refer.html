I'm writing this mail to bring to your notice an initiative called 'Project Awaaz' which is an attempt to crowd-source audio content for the 15 million visually impaired people in India.

It's a website where you can record poems and short-stories (in English, Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil and Marathi) and add to the repository by spending less than 5 minutes of your time daily. These will then be distributed to blind schools and NGOs, bringing about positive impact in the lives of many. 

Log on to www.projectawaaz.org and be a part of the change!
